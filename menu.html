Menu Notes: Each pound(lb) of meat/seafood in the bag will come with 1 corn and 1 potato. See below for example.

Ex: 1/2 lb Snow Crab comes with 0 corn and 0 potato.

Ex: 1 lb Snow Crab comes with 1 corn and 1 potato.

Ex: 1 lb Snow Crab and 1/2 Sausage comes with 1 corn and 2 potato.

Ex: 1 lb Snow Crab, 1/2 lb Sausage, and 1/2 lb Shrimp(no head) comes with 2 corn and 2 potato.
